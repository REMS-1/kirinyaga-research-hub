Research Policy 2024

Eligibility Framework for
Institutional Grants

Our grants support groundbreaking research that aligns with the sustainable development goals of the Kirinyaga region and the broader academic community.

verified

Who is Eligible

  • Full-time faculty members at recognized institutions with a proven track record of scholarly publication.
  • Doctoral candidates in their final year of study, provided they have an institutional endorsement.
  • Independent researchers with a minimum of 5 years of specialized experience in their respective fields.
  • Registered Non-Profit Organizations (NGOs) focused on regional socioeconomic research.
block

Ineligibility Criteria

Operational Exclusions

  • Current employees of the Kirinyaga Research Hub.
  • Researchers with outstanding technical reports from previous grants.
  • For-profit commercial entities and private firms.

Project Exclusions

  • Projects lacking a clear regional impact or data.
  • Research that duplicates existing government-funded studies.
  • Capital expenditure requests unrelated to research.
