Application Tips

check_circle

Start Early

Begin your application at least 6 weeks before the deadline to allow for institutional approvals.

check_circle

Align with Funder Priorities

Read the call for proposals carefully and align your abstract with the stated objectives.

check_circle

Quantify Impact

Include measurable outcomes and a clear theory of change. Funders want to see tangible results.

check_circle

Budget Transparency

Provide a detailed, justifiable budget. Unexplained line items are the most common rejection reason.

check_circle

Contact the Advisory Team

Our research office can review your proposal before submission. Use the Contact page to book a session.
