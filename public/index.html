Our Purpose

The Archival Mission

To document, analyze, and resolve the socio-economic and technical challenges of Kirinyaga County by facilitating high-impact collaborative research and knowledge exchange between academia and the community.

Strategic Vision

Becoming the premier digital repository and catalyst for intellectual development in the region—where every local problem finds a scholarly solution through a unified ecosystem of data and discovery.
